OUR STORY


Our Founder and CEO, Maduabuchi Gift is a frontend developer and has always loved working with tech. Despite her love for the field, she often experienced discrimination associated with been a woman in a male dominated industry.

Founded in 2019, Young Girls Coder began to bridge the gap of females in the tech industry. Gift wanted to form strong connections and female friendships so she decided to host her own event - one specifically for girls in tech.

Gift and the team of Young Girls Coder believe that no one should miss out on education for any reason - whether socioeconomic or other, which is why Young Girls Coder focuses on making tech accessible.
